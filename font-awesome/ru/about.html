Roborzoid

Roborzoid platform allows:

  • Control devices, apps or services by voice
  • Achieve fast integration for smart home devices into a single voice-controlled system
  • Simplify the usage of devices for the end user
  • Enter the trending market of devices, apps and services controlled by voice;
  • Keep pace with competitors using voice control technologies
  • Understand context with the help of computer vision

From the users' perspective:

  • Get rid of the burden of searching the required serice or an app in big lists, menus, screens as it is in the standard keyboard/mouse or sensor input devices
  • Get rid of using eyes or arms for controlling devices, apps or services
  • Speed up information search
  • Have a personal assistant and companion
  • Simplify interaction with third-party services, concentrate on the task - and not on vendors and competitive advantages. Services range depending on user preferences and community dynamics.
